About Us

Home Carer About

We think your little ones justify the very best care.

We are a family-operated company. We are a locally based company providing disability services in Gold Coast and the surrounding area. We focus on providing individualised personal care and pride ourselves in providing the best quality care and support to our clients. We understand that it can be a challenging situation for our clients and loved ones, and we are here to listen to your journey, challenges and work together with you and your families to help develop a plan to support you and empower you where you can achieve optimal health and outcome you desire. We are here for you and will provide care involving you and your family and ensure that we work with you to get the best match of the staff.

Our Values

Dignity and Respect- We will provide care always maintaining your dignity. We will respect you and your choices and opinions and work together with you.

  • Caring and Compassionate- We will be caring and compassionate throughout our service.
  • Honesty – We will always be honest with you and maintain the trust relationship.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide highest quality care and support in partnership with our clients and families focusing on empowering our clients where they can achieve their well desired outcome.
